Tony Stark

Iron Man Iron Man is the superhero persona of Anthony Edward Stark, a businessman and engineer who runs Stark Industries. In the comics, Tony Stark is the wealthy son of industrialist and weapons manufacturer Howard Stark. Tony is a genius with a brilliant mind for technology and inventions. In the 2008 film Iron Man, Tony Stark is a billionaire weapons developer who is held captive in an Afghan cave. After barely surviving an attack on his convoy, he creates a weaponized suit of armor to fight evil.
